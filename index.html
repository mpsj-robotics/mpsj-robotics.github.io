Michael Power/St. Joseph is the largest high school in the TCDSB and has one of the highest secondary school populations in Toronto.


About Us

Since 2006, the Michael Power/St. Joseph Robotics Team has been putting its' greatest minds to work in the annual Skills Canada Mobile Robotics Competition. Members work together, using their skills in 3D modelling, electronics, and programming to create robots. In the past, the MPSJ Robotics Team has won many awards for its' exemplary performance.
